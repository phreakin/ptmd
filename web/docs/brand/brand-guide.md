# Paper Trail MD Brand Guide

## Brand
Paper Trail MD

## Tagline
We Follow the Story Until It Breaks.
...Or Until It Stops Making Sense.

## Brand Positioning
Paper Trail MD produces hard-hitting but funny mini-documentaries focused on social, cultural, and political stories.
The brand voice should feel investigative, skeptical, sharp, and entertaining without losing credibility.

## Palette
- Midnight Black: #0B0C10
- Paper White: #F5F5F3
- Deep Navy: #1C2A39
- Slate Gray: #2F3A40
- Evidence Red: #C1121F
- Highlighter Yellow: #FFD60A
- Forensic Teal: #2EC4B6
- Muted Gold: #BFA181

## Logo Direction
The logo blends crime and medicine:
- fingerprint
- caduceus / medical symbol
- evidence stamp
- dark investigative styling

## Thumbnail Direction
- big bold text
- one emotional face or focal image
- dark gritty background
- use yellow/red accents sparingly
- text examples:
  - THE HIDDEN TRUTH?
  - WHAT REALLY HAPPENED?
  - CASE FILE

## Overlay Direction
- Lower thirds:
  - red for case files
  - yellow for highlights
  - teal for data/timelines
- Evidence stamp used sparingly on key reveal moments
- Use glitch, grit, and subtle forensic textures
- Watermarks should be subtle and non-intrusive

## Social Intro Direction
- YouTube: 5-8 second cinematic intro with logo and tagline
- TikTok/Reels/Shorts: 1-2 second fast brand flash
- Facebook: 2-3 second lighter intro
- X: minimal or no intro, quick visual identification only

## Posting Rhythm
- Friday: teaser clip
- Saturday: teaser on short-form platforms
- Sunday: full YouTube documentary
- Monday-Thursday: staggered clips across platforms
