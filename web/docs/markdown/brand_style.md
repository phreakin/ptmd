## 🎨 Color Usage
- **Primary** for buttons, links, and key highlights
- **Secondary** for warnings, errors, and important notices
- **Success** for success messages and confirmations
- **Warning** for cautionary messages and alerts
- **Danger** for critical errors and destructive actions
- **Info** for informational messages and tips
- **Neutral** for disabled states and less important elements
- **Background** for the main background color of the app
- **Foreground** for text and primary content
- **Border** for borders and dividers
- **Accent** for accents and highlights
- **Highlight** for important highlights and callouts
- **Shadow** for shadows and depth
- **Dark** for dark themes and elements
- **Light** for light themes and elements
- **Grays** for various UI elements, backgrounds, and text


## 🎨 Color Language

- 🔥 **Harsh yellows** (#FFD400 / #FFEA00)
- ❤️ **Deep reds** (#C1121F / #FF3B30)
- 🖤 **Charcoal / near‑black backgrounds** (#0A0A0A / #111111)
- 🟠 **Rusty oranges** (#F97316 / #D35400)
- ⚫ **High contrast with white text** (#FFFFFF)
- 💀 **Occasional use of grays** (#7F7F7F / #B3B3B3) for secondary elements

## 🔠 Typography Tone

- **Proper Case** (not all caps, not lowercase)
- **Bold, stamped, distressed energy**
- **Short, punchy lines**
- **Documentary‑style phrasing**
- **“Case file” / “confidential” vibes**

---

## 🧱 Layout Feel

- **Blocky sections**
- **Strong dividers**
- **Minimal whitespace**
- **High contrast**
- **“This is evidence” energy**

---

## 🎭 Tone

- **Raw**
- **Honest**
- **Slightly chaotic**
- **Cinematic**
- **A little dark**
- **A little funny**
- **Zero corporate polish**
- **"The real deal"**
- **"Torn pages and chaos feel"**

---

## 🧨 Profanity (Light, Intentional)

- Allowed
- Not overdone
- Just enough to feel human and real  
- Used for emphasis, not shock value
